# Strategic Roadmap

**We are building the infrastructure for the next generation of digital finance.**

Our roadmap is focused on delivering a secure, scalable, and decentralized trading experience that rivals centralized exchanges in utility while superior in security.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Q2 2025)

_Establishing the Core Infrastructure._

<Steps>
  <Step title="Protocol Launch">Deployment of audited smart contracts to Mainnet.</Step>
  <Step title="Liquidity Formation">Initial liquidity bootstrapping via Uniswap to ensure stable market depth.</Step>
  <Step title="Public Access">Opening the platform to the public for permissionless trading.</Step>
</Steps>

## Phase 2: Growth & Ecosystem (Q3 2025)

_Expanding Utility and Adoption._

- **Payment Rail Expansion**: Integrating support for a wider range of global fiat payment methods.
- **Ambassador Program**: Onboarding regional partners to support local communities.
- **Reputation System V1**: Launch of the Soulbound RepToken to establish on-chain trust.

## Phase 3: Scaling & Optimization (Q4 2025)

_Enhancing Performance and Reach._

- **Exchange Listings**: Increasing token accessibility through major DEX listings.
- **Staking Activation**: Enabling revenue sharing for protocol participants.
- **Arbitration Dao**: Decentralizing the dispute resolution process.
- **Cross-Chain Deployment**: Expanding support to additional EVM-compatible networks.
