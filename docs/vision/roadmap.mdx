# Roadmap

Our development roadmap focuses on building a robust, decentralized trading platform.

## Q2 2025 — Foundation & Launch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Documentation & Tokenomics">Finalize project documentation and economic model</Step>
  <Step title="Token Presale">Launch PEXFI token presale with whitelist mechanism</Step>
  <Step title="Liquidity Bootstrap">Create initial liquidity pool on Uniswap (PEXFI/ETH)</Step>
  <Step title="Mainnet Deployment">Deploy production contracts and switch frontend to mainnet</Step>
  <Step title="Community Launch">Begin community onboarding and initial marketing</Step>
</Steps>

## Q3 2025 — Growth & Adoption

- Integrate more fiat payment systems
- Launch ambassador and affiliate programs
- Begin badge-based NFT reputation incentives
- Partner with fiat payment platforms and local agents
- First post-launch feature upgrades and UI polish

## Q4 2025 — Expansion & Utility

- List PEXFI on additional DEXs and aggregators
- Launch staking and arbitrator selection mechanisms
- Expand arbitrator pool through manual review
- Launch analytics and user tools
- Expand support to additional networks
