# Trading with Confidence

Every trade on PEXFI is powered by a **Smart Contract Escrow**. This ensures that tokens are safely locked until the payment is confirmed, protecting both the buyer and the seller.

## How a Deal Works

A deal is the journey from finding an offer to receiving your tokens. It follows a simple, secure process:

### 1. Initiation
When a buyer accepts an offer, a unique **Deal Contract** is created on the blockchain. This contract acts as a neutral "vault" for the trade.

### 2. Escrow Funding
The seller deposits the tokens into the Deal Contract. Once funded, the tokens are **locked**—the seller cannot withdraw them, and the buyer cannot take them yet. This provides proof to the buyer that the seller has the assets.

### 3. Payment & Confirmation
The buyer sends the payment (e.g., via bank transfer) directly to the seller using the agreed-upon method. Once sent, the buyer marks the deal as "Payment Sent."

### 4. Release
The seller verifies the receipt of funds in their account and clicks **Release**. The Deal Contract instantly transfers the tokens from the escrow vault to the buyer's wallet.

## Your Role in a Deal

Whether you are buying or selling, the process is streamlined to keep you informed at every step.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="As a Buyer">
    - **Accept an Offer**: Choose a deal and enter your amount.
    - **Wait for Funding**: Ensure the seller has locked the tokens in escrow before sending any money.
    - **Send Payment**: Use the provided payment details to send your fiat.
    - **Confirm Payment**: Mark the deal as "Payment Sent" on PEXFI to protect your claim.
    - **Receive Tokens**: Once the seller releases, the tokens appear in your wallet.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="As a Seller">
    - **Review the Deal**: See who wants to trade with you.
    - **Fund Escrow**: Deposit your tokens into the deal contract to start the clock.
    - **Verify Payment**: Check your bank or payment app for the incoming transfer.
    - **Release Tokens**: Click "Release" only after you have confirmed the funds are safely in your possession.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Secure On-Chain Messaging

Every deal includes a private, **on-chain message board**. This is your official line of communication:

- **Coordinate**: Share payment details or ask questions directly within the deal.
- **Evidence**: In the rare case of a dispute, these messages serve as a permanent record for mediators.
- **Privacy**: Your conversation is tied to the deal and remains accessible to the parties involved.

<Note>
  **Pro Tip**: Always keep your communication inside the PEXFI deal chat. If a dispute arises, mediators can only review messages sent through the platform.
</Note>

## Cancellation & Protection

- **Flexibility**: Deals can be cancelled by either party if the trade hasn't progressed too far.
- **Safety**: If a buyer cancels after funding, the tokens are returned to the seller.
- **Disputes**: If a disagreement occurs after payment is sent, our mediation system ensures a fair resolution based on on-chain evidence.
