# Trust the Process: Fair Arbitration

In any peer-to-peer marketplace, disputes can happen. PEXFI handles these moments differently than traditional platforms. Instead of slow, opaque "support tickets," we use a decentralized, **UMA-style protocol** that ensures every conflict is resolved quickly and fairly based on truth.

## Why Our System is Fair

Our arbitration is built on powerful economic incentives that protect you:

- **Economic Honesty**: Arbitrators (mediators) are financially incentivized to make the correct, honest decision. If they attempt to cheat or favor one side unfairly, they risk losing their own staked assets.
- **Checked and Balanced**: No single person has the final word. Every proposed resolution can be challenged by other participants. This constant oversight ensures that only the truth prevails.
- **Speed through Efficiency**: Because mediators want to earn rewards and avoid challenges, they are motivated to review evidence and propose fair outcomes as quickly as possible.

---

## When to Open a Dispute

You should initiate a dispute if a trade cannot be completed normally. Common reasons include:

- **Payment Discrepancies**: You sent the payment, but the seller hasn't released the tokens.
- **Unresponsive Counterparty**: Your trade partner has stopped communicating during an active deal.
- **Verification Issues**: The payment received doesn't match the agreed amount or the sender's details.

<Note>
  **Patience First**: We recommend allowing a reasonable amount of time for bank transfers to clear and for your partner to respond before escalating to a dispute.
</Note>

## How to Win a Dispute

In our decentralized system, **evidence is everything**. To ensure a favorable outcome, you must provide clear proof of your actions:

### Key Evidence to Provide
- **Payment Receipts**: Clear screenshots or PDFs of your bank transfer or payment app confirmation.
- **On-Chain Chat**: Keep all your coordination inside the PEXFI deal messenger. Our mediators use these logs as the primary source of truth.
- **Timestamps**: Ensure your evidence shows exactly when the payment was initiated and completed.

### The Resolution Process
Once a dispute is active, the process is straightforward:
1. **Evidence Review**: Mediators examine the deal messages and the proof provided by both parties.
2. **Proposed Outcome**: A mediator proposes to either **Release** the tokens to the buyer or **Cancel** them back to the seller.
3. **Final Settlement**: After a short window for potential challenges, the Deal Contract automatically executes the decision.

## Security & Protection

- **Vault Security**: During a dispute, the tokens remain safely locked in the Deal Contract's escrow. Neither the mediator nor PEXFI can ever take these funds for themselves.
- **Fraud Prevention**: Our system is designed to make fraud unprofitable. Scammers cannot win against clear, on-chain evidence and economically motivated mediators.

**With PEXFI, you aren't just trading with a stranger—you're trading within a system designed to protect the truth.**
