# Accessing PEXFI

PEXFI is a decentralized, Web3-native protocol. On our platform, your wallet is your identity, your login, and your secure vault.

We prioritize absolute self-custody. This means you retain full control over your assets at all times. PEXFI does not store passwords, emails, or any personal data that could compromise your security or privacy.

## Connecting Your Wallet

PEXFI is designed to be compatible with any standard Web3 wallet. Whether you prefer a browser extension, a mobile app, or a hardware wallet, you can seamlessly connect to the platform.

### How to Connect

1.  **Click Connect**: Locate and click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top right corner of the application.
2.  **Select Your Provider**: Choose your preferred wallet provider from the list. If your wallet is not explicitly listed, you can usually connect via **WalletConnect**.
3.  **Authorize**: Follow the prompts on your device to authorize the connection. This process establishes your identity on the platform without ever sharing your private keys.

<Note>
  **Security First**: PEXFI will *never* ask for your seed phrase or private keys. Every action on the platform that involves your funds will require your explicit approval and signature within your wallet.
</Note>

## Why No Username or Password?

Traditional account systems rely on centralized databases which are vulnerable to hacks, data breaches, and administrative interference. By using cryptographic keys:

- **Sovereignty**: You are the only person who can authorize transactions or manage your account.
- **Security**: There is no central database of passwords to be compromised.
- **Independence**: PEXFI cannot lock you out of your own funds because we do not hold your keys.

By connecting your wallet, you are utilizing the most secure and transparent method available for interacting with financial protocols.
