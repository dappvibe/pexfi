## Connect Your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open PEXFI">Navigate to the PEXFI app</Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect">Find the wallet button in the top right</Step>
  <Step title="Select Wallet">Choose your wallet provider</Step>
  <Step title="Approve Connection">Confirm in your wallet extension</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>For enhanced security, consider using a hardware wallet like Ledger or Trezor.</Tip>

## Browse the Marketplace

Once connected, explore available offers:

1. **Filter by token** — USDT, USDC, DAI, etc.
2. **Select your currency** — USD, EUR, RUB, and more
3. **Choose payment method** — Bank transfer, cash, mobile payments
4. **Review trader reputation** — Check completion rates

## Your First Trade

### Buying Crypto

1. Find a sell offer that matches your needs
2. Enter the amount you want to buy
3. Accept the offer
4. Wait for seller to fund escrow
5. Send fiat payment as instructed
6. Confirm payment in the deal
7. Receive your crypto

### Selling Crypto

1. Create a sell offer with your terms
2. Wait for a buyer
3. Fund the escrow when a deal begins
4. Receive fiat payment
5. Release tokens to buyer

## Safety Tips

<Warning>
  Always keep communication within the platform. Off-chain messages are not protected by dispute resolution.
</Warning>

- **Verify payment** before releasing tokens
- **Start small** with new trading partners
- **Check reputation** of counterparties
- **Document everything** in the deal chat
