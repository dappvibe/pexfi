# Trading Guide

This guide walks you through the complete trading process on PEXFI.

## Buying Crypto

### Step 1: Find an Offer

Browse the marketplace and filter by:

- Token you want to buy
- Your fiat currency
- Preferred payment method
- Trade amount within your range

### Step 2: Review the Seller

Before accepting, check:

| Factor          | What to Look For              |
| --------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Trade Count     | More trades = more experience |
| Completion Rate | Should be 95%+ ideally        |
| Response Time   | Fast responders are reliable  |
| Terms           | Read their trading conditions |

### Step 3: Accept the Offer

1. Enter your desired amount
2. Review the exchange rate
3. Click **Accept**
4. Approve the transaction in your wallet

A new deal contract is created on-chain.

### Step 4: Wait for Funding

The seller must deposit tokens into escrow. You'll see the status change to **Funded**.

<Note>If the seller doesn't fund within a reasonable time, you can cancel the deal.</Note>

### Step 5: Send Payment

1. Use the payment method specified in the offer
2. Follow any instructions from the seller
3. Send the exact fiat amount agreed

### Step 6: Mark as Paid

Once you've sent payment:

1. Click **Payment Sent**
2. Confirm the transaction

This notifies the seller to check their account.

### Step 7: Receive Tokens

When the seller confirms receipt of payment, they release the tokens. You'll see them in your wallet.

## Selling Crypto

### Step 1: Create an Offer

Go to **My Offers** â†’ **Create Offer**:

1. Select the token you're selling
2. Choose the fiat currency
3. Set your price (fixed or with margin)
4. Define minimum and maximum limits
5. List accepted payment methods
6. Add your trading terms

### Step 2: Wait for Buyers

Your offer appears in the marketplace. Buyers can accept it at any time.

### Step 3: Fund the Escrow

When someone accepts your offer:

1. Review the deal details
2. Click **Fund Escrow**
3. Approve the token transfer

Your tokens are now locked until the trade completes.

### Step 4: Receive Payment

The buyer sends fiat to your account. Verify:

- Correct amount received
- Payment from the expected sender
- No pending or reversible status

### Step 5: Release Tokens

Once payment is confirmed in your account:

1. Click **Release Tokens**
2. Confirm the transaction

The buyer receives their crypto.
