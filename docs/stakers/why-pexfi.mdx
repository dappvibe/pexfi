# Redefining Trading Sovereignty

For too long, crypto trading has been held captive by centralized intermediaries. We believe in a different future—one where you are in total control of your financial life.

## Why PEXFI is Different

Centralized exchanges (CEXs) are a compromise. They force you to trade security for convenience, and privacy for access. PEXFI offers a better way:

### 1. Absolute Self-Custody
On PEXFI, you are the only person who holds the keys to your assets. We never touch your funds, meaning there's no risk of exchange runs, insolvency, or account freezes.

### 2. Privacy as a Priority
Your financial history and identity should be your business, not a centralized database's risk. PEXFI requires no KYC, allowing you to trade securely using only your wallet.

### 3. Trust the Code, Not the Company
Traditional exchanges ask you to trust a corporation. We ask you to trust open-source, auditable code. Every trade on PEXFI is governed by a transparent smart contract escrow.

We are building for a future where:
- **Financial Privacy** is protected by default.
- **Asset Security** is guaranteed by mathematics and cryptography.
- **Censorship Resistance** ensures that no central authority can block your access to the global economy.
