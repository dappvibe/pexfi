# Stake to Mediate: The Guardians of Truth

**Earn yield by securing the PEXFI marketplace through decentralized arbitration.**

In a P2P world, disputes are inevitable. PEXFI doesn't use "support tickets" or central admins. Instead, we use an **Optimistic Dispute Resolution** system powered by $stPEXFI$ holders.

## How it Works: UMA-Style Arbitration

PEXFI employs a game-theoretic model similar to the UMA Protocol to ensure that truth is the most profitable outcome.

1.  **Dispute Triggered**: If a buyer or seller disagrees on a payment, either party can trigger a dispute. **This is free for the user.**
2.  **Proposed Resolution**: Any $stPEXFI$ holder with a sufficient stake can propose a resolution: **Release** (to buyer) or **Cancel** (back to seller). No other outcomes are possible.
3.  **The Challenge Window**: After a proposal is made, a challenge window opens. Any other $stPEXFI$ holder can challenge the proposal if they believe it is unfair or incorrect.
4.  **Community Vote (UMA)**: If challenged, the dispute enters a voting phase where $stPEXFI$ holders (and potentially the broader UMA ecosystem) vote on the outcome. 
5.  **Slash & Reward**: Honest participants are rewarded with a portion of protocol fees. Those who propose or support a fraudulent resolution have their stakes slashed.

## The Role of $stPEXFI$

By staking your $PEXFI$, you receive $stPEXFI$, which grants you the power to:

- **Propose Resolutions**: Act as the first line of arbitration.
- **Challenge Deception**: Protect the integrity of the marketplace and earn from slashed stakes.
- **Vote on Truth**: Participate in the final oracle consensus.
