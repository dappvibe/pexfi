# Secure the Grid, Earn Rewards

In a truly decentralized P2P marketplace, truth is the only authority. PEXFI doesn't rely on central admins; instead, we empower **$stPEXFI$ holders** to act as the "immune system" of the platform through our decentralized mediation system.

## How it Works: The Path of Truth

When you stake your PEXFI, you don't just earn yield—you gain the power to arbitrate disputes. Our system is designed so that being honest is always the most profitable action.

### 1. The Dispute
If a buyer or seller disagrees on a payment, a dispute is triggered. The trade is paused, and the escrow remains locked.

### 2. Proposing a Resolution
Any $stPEXFI$ holder with a sufficient stake can review the on-chain evidence (messages, payment proofs) and propose a resolution: **Release** (to buyer) or **Cancel** (back to seller).

### 3. The Challenge Window
Once a resolution is proposed, other stakers have a window to challenge it if they believe it's incorrect. This creates a powerful incentive for the first mediator to be absolutely fair.

### 4. The Reward
Honest mediators who propose or support the correct resolution are rewarded with a portion of the protocol's transaction fees. This is **Real Yield** earned by providing a critical service to the ecosystem.
