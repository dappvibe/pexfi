# A Strategic Roadmap for the Future

PEXFI is more than just a platform; it's a mission to build the world's most secure and sovereign peer-to-peer exchange. We are following a phased approach to ensure stability, security, and true decentralization.

## Foundation & Launch (Completed)
The first steps in our journey have laid the groundwork for a robust ecosystem:
- **Audited Escrow Contracts**: Deploying battle-tested code to ensure your funds are always secure.
- **Liquidity Bootstrapping**: Establishing deep and stable liquidity for $PEXFI.
- **Open Trading**: Launching the platform for everyone to trade without barriers.

## Ecosystem Growth & Utility
Our next steps focus on expanding the platform's reach and enhancing the experience for every user:
- **Universal Payment Options**: Seamlessly integrating diverse fiat methods from around the globe.
- **The Merchant Network**: Empowering local liquidity providers with specialized tools and programs.
- **Reputation SBTs**: Activating on-chain, soulbound verification to build trust without compromise.

## Scaling & Staking
Scaling the protocol while rewarding those who support it:
- **Global Exchange Presence**: Ensuring $PEXFI is accessible across major platforms.
- **Real Yield Rewards**: Activating the revenue-sharing model to reward long-term stakers.
- **Multi-Chain Expansion**: Deploying PEXFI across the broader EVM ecosystem for maximum flexibility.

## Towards Complete Sovereignty
Our ultimate goal is to become a fully decentralized, community-governed protocol:
- **Full DAO Governance**: Putting the keys to the kingdom in the hands of the $stPEXFI$ holders.
- **Privacy First**: Integrating zero-knowledge proofs to protect your transaction details.
- **The Mobile Ecosystem**: Bringing sovereign trading to every device with dedicated apps.
